About Us

The Aari Zone Government Communication Affairs is dedicated to providing information about the activities of the Aari Zone Government, gathering community feedback, disseminating news to broadcast media and social media, and serving as an archive of government activities for future use.

Services

  • Providing information about the Aari Zone Government's activities
  • Gathering and conveying community feedback to the government
  • Sending news to broadcast media and social media
  • Maintaining an archive of government activities for future use
